About Me

Hey there! I'm Kyhl Blake Hines, a passionate software developer and content creator hailing from the vibrant state of Alabama. With a background as an ADHD military brat, I've experienced life through the lens of constant change and adaptability. My journey started on the soccer field, where I dominated for 12 years. However, when I hit a roadblock in college, I found myself taking an unexpected detour and joined the army. After rigorous training and acquiring the skills of an Information Technology Specialist, including the prestigious CCNA, CASP, and CEH certifications, I became known as Mr. Wifi, the Como GOD.


Following my time in the army, I ventured into the contractor space, starting from a humble help desk tech and working my way up to become a Tier 1 Network Admin. Those years tested my resilience and shaped me into the determined individual I am today. Now, in my late 20s, I'm embarking on a new chapter in my life, driven by a burning desire to combine my love for technology and content creation. Imagine a motovlogger/semi-pro gamer/streamer who knows their way around software development. This unique blend of skills allows me to create captivating content while also crafting innovative software solutions.


With my passion for documenting life's adventures and my extensive technical knowledge, I'm excited to share my journey with the world. Through my website, I aim to provide valuable insights, engaging content, and even a killer home movie to look back on. Join me as I delve into the fascinating realm of software development, exploring the limitless possibilities it offers. Let's create, innovate, and embark on this incredible journey together.
